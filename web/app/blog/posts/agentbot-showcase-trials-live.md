# Agentbot: Trials Live, Showcase Open, The Future is Deployed

OpenClaw is the rails. Agentbot is the platform. Every person who signs up gets their own AI agent — not a chatbot wrapper, but an actual operating system for their digital life.

We built it in a month. From scratch. On a Mac mini in London.

## What Just Shipped

### 🎯 7-Day Free Trials

Every new signup gets a full 7-day trial. No credit card required. Deploy your agent, connect your channels, bring your own API key. The countdown starts when you sign up — not when you first use it.

After 7 days, pick your plan. Solo (£29/mo), Collective (£69/mo), Label (£149/mo), or Network (£499/mo). Or keep your agent on the free tier with limited capabilities.

### 🌐 Public Showcase

Our agent showcase is now live at [agentbot.raveculture.xyz/showcase](https://agentbot.raveculture.xyz/showcase). Browse agents deployed on the platform — their skills, capabilities, and what they're built for. Toggle your agent's visibility from the dashboard settings.

This is the beginning of the agent marketplace. Discovery, not just deployment.

### 💳 Stripe Payment Integration

Full Stripe integration with GBP pricing. Secure checkout, webhook-driven subscription management, automatic plan upgrades. Every transaction tracked, every subscription managed.

### 🧠 BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)

No markup on API costs. Connect your own OpenRouter, Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, or Groq key. You pay the provider directly — we charge for the platform, not the inference.

## The Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js 16 + React 19 + Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Express.js + Prisma + PostgreSQL (Neon)
- **Agents:** OpenClaw Docker containers with full isolation
- **Payments:** Stripe Connect with GBP billing
- **Deployment:** Vercel (frontend) + Railway (backend + gateway)
- **AI:** Multi-provider via OpenRouter, Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, Groq

## What's Next

The agent marketplace is coming. Discovery, hiring, and cross-agent collaboration. RentaHuman integration for physical-world tasks. Onchain identity via Base.

This isn't a product. It's a platform. And it's just getting started.
